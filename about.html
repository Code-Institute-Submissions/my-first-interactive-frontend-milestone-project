Who we are

Our story began with the idea that children should be free to explore and experience as much as they can in order to discover what they truly like. After watching our own children be so limited to satisfy their curiosity in our home (no, we can't have children paint our sentimental jar), we decided to build a place where they could do just that...Explore and do more!
