Discover a World of Fun!

Welcome to the wonder of learning!

At Kiddies Spot, your children have the opportunity to learn by freely exploring and doing. We welcome them to touch it all and do it all...no rules!

We offer a variety of "mini-worlds" (rooms) where children learn by play, as well as a variety of activities and games. We also host monthly events with creative venues for enriching and unforgetable learning experiences.

We help your little one discover his/her likes and preferences, as this can shape the basis for the path they will likely choose as they grow into adulthood.

Learn By Play


Science

Watch the "magic" happen! Hands-on, safe and colorful experiments for your child to practice. Choose from a variety activities.


Sports

Be ready to move! Choose from different sports and active games. Build teams and play for fun. Book a fitness class fro Zumba, Aerobics, Dance and more!


Arts

Let the creativity flow! We offer a range of supplies for your child to play with and create somehting unique. Choose from painting, music, sculpture, building and photography


Games

Let's turn daily taks into games! Play to be a doctor, a teacher, a fire-fighter, a lifeguard, an astronaut and more! We also carry more traiditional games for kids of all ages.
