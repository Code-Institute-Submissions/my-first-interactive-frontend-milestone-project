Events

Week June 1st - June 7th

Recycling

  • Date: Saturday, June 06
  • Time: 2:00 PM (EST)
  • Where: KiddosSpot's Backyard
  • Maximum capacity:40 children. Signup now!
  • Cost: $0

Come join us for our semi-annual event where your little ones will learn the importance of recycling and fun ways to incorporate it to their daily habits.

We will have our recycling expert "Ricky Recycle" illustrate where reclyced items go afer being picked up

Your little one will also venture in discovering ways to reuse items

Bring your previously washed glass, plstic, cardboard items for our practical exercise/contest.

Let's Plan a Tree!

  • Date:Sunday, June 07
  • Time: 9:00 AM (EST)
  • Where: Holly Ridge High School
  • Maximum capacity:40 children. Signup now!
  • Cost: $0

Holly Ridge Tree Foundation will take your little one to the wonder of trees. In this interactive event they will teach the basics of planting and caring for a trees. Your child will plant a tree at location and do some hands-on caring tasks for trees previously planted

We will also do a walk-by the different trees at our location and their benefits

Your child will be given the optional task to plant his/her own tree on his home's backyard. We will supply the seeds and some soil
